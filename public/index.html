What's inside

Everything a student needs
to crack placements

From daily coding streaks to company-wise questions — UTPT puts it all in one clean dashboard.

📊
Live Score Dashboard
See your composite placement score update every 2 hours — weighted across coding, contests, and open source contributions.
🏆
College Leaderboard
Rank yourself against batchmates in real time. Filter by branch, batch, or overall to benchmark where you stand.
🤖
Smart Nudges
Daily 8 AM alerts push-notify and email students who are falling behind — before it becomes a problem.
❓
Curated Questions
Company-tagged DSA questions with difficulty filters. Track which ones you've solved and what's still pending.
📚
Resource Library
Trainers upload prep material — PDFs, links, videos — organized by topic so nothing gets buried in a WhatsApp group.
🛡️
Admin & Trainer Panel
Bulk-import students, manage accounts, view per-student analytics, and control resources — all from one admin view.

Integrations

How it works

Up and running in minutes

1
Verify with OTP
Use your GLA email to receive a one-time code. No password needed on first access.
2
Link your handles
Add your LeetCode username, Codeforces handle, and GitHub profile in your settings.
3
Watch your score grow
UTPT syncs your activity every 2 hours and recalculates your placement readiness score.
4
Climb the leaderboard
Compete with your batch, spot weak areas, and get ready before placement season hits.
