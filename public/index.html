Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

From live platform sync to automated leaderboards — UTPT gives training cells total visibility over every student's journey.

Live Performance Tracking
Auto-sync scores from LeetCode, Codeforces, CodeChef and HackerRank. Real-time leaderboards update every 2 hours — no manual entry.
GitHub Activity Sync
Pull commit counts, repo stats, and contribution graphs automatically. See who's building real projects alongside DSA practice.
Role-Based Dashboards
Students see personal progress. Trainers manage their batches. Admins get a bird's-eye view across all departments and cohorts.
Smart Leaderboards
Weighted scoring across platforms — DSA, GitHub, and soft metrics combined into a single campus rank with full history.
Resource Library
Trainers upload notes, sheets, and videos. Students access them with one click. Everything organised by topic and batch.
Smart Nudge System
Automatically flags students who haven't solved problems in 3+ days. Daily 8AM alerts help trainers act before gaps become habits.
